About

The internet is a free source of information and we want to take advantage of this free technology. Bloodlife provides an online platform that connects people who are in need of blood with banks and hospitals. For now we don't cater blood donors, but maybe we will in the future. We believe this will really help everyone. We believe we would be able to help Cebuanos in our little ways.
