About

The internet is a free source of information and we want to take advantage of this free technology. Bloodlife provides an online platform that connects people who are in need of blood with donors. We believe this will really help everyone. We believe we would be able to help our fellow Filipinos in our little ways.

Be A Donor

Message us to be a voluntary blood donor.

By registering you will automatically be added to the active list of blood donors, who will then receive Facebook notifications when there is a match for your blood type.

Message us with this format 'REGISTER < Your Blood Type >', for example : REGISTER O+

Inquire

Please choose the blood type that you are looking for and provide your contact details. Your inquiry will be broadcasted to the kind hearted blood donors and they will reach you from the contact details that you provide below.
